Our Vision

Nomcebo Community Development Projects (NCDP) is a registered Non-Profit Organization with the Department of Social Development under NPO Act 71 of 1997. Established in June 2006, NCDP operates in Mayfield, within the boundaries of Daveyton township, South Africa.

We envision a community where every individual has access to the resources and opportunities they need to thrive. Our goal is to create sustainable solutions that empower community members and foster long-term positive change.

To provide all citizens with quality healthcare and serve the community with exemplary home-based care, grounded in moral values that nurture successful community members.

Upskill the community in and around our immediate surrounding with accredited Skills Programs

Our Mission

Empowering communities to support older persons and people living with HIV/AIDS in distress

Implementing proper planning and wellness programs through inclusive daily activities

Utilizing all available resources to enhance health and wellness by developing skills, knowledge, values, and attitudes for technological advancement

Identifying challenges and implementing strategic interventions

Our Values

Respect and Dignity: Serving the community with respect, honor, and dignity

Empathy and Warmth: Receiving every child and vulnerable person with empathy and warmth

Professionalism: Maintaining courtesy, willingness, and discipline in all interactions

As an organization, we are committed to providing essential social and health support to our communities while developing participants' skills and knowledge.
