Our Story

Nomcebo Community Development Projects (NCDP) is a registered Non-Profit Organization with the Department of Social Development under NPO Act 71 of 1997. Established in June 2006, NCDP operates in Mayfield, within the boundaries of Daveyton township, South Africa.

Our community faces significant challenges including high unemployment rates, poverty, hunger, low literacy levels, crime, and gender-based violence. The HIV/AIDS and COVID-19 pandemics have severely impacted residents, creating dysfunctional households and increased unemployment due to economic downturns.

These pandemics have separated families and contributed to increased gender-based violence against women and children. Recognizing these challenges, Ms. Hazel Mnguni established NCDP as a center of hope, joy, and restoration for vulnerable community members.

Since inception, NCDP has established four project sites and served approximately 1,569 community members. Through our social and health programs, we have provided care and support to 394 households while contributing to personal growth and community development through training and skills development programs.
