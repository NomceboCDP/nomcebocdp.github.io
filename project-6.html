Senior Care Program

Started: November 2021 - Ongoing Location: Nomcebo Elderly Center & home visits Seniors served: 150+

Project Overview

The Senior Care Program supports elderly community members through social activities, health services, and practical assistance, helping them maintain independence and quality of life.

Implementation

We partnered with local supermarkets to donate grocery parcels to the elderly, and those in need within our community.

The program includes home-based care support for the elderly in need.

Results and Impact

Participants report improved mental health and reduced loneliness. The program has helped identify and address health issues early through regular check-ups.

Family caregivers receive respite support, and the intergenerational component has fostered greater understanding between age groups in the community.

Future Plans

We're expanding to include a transportation service for medical appointments and adding memory care support for seniors with dementia. Plans include developing an assisted living facility for those needing more intensive care.

Get Involved

Volunteers can assist with meal delivery, companionship visits, or leading activities at the senior center. Donations help provide meals, medical supplies, and transportation services.
